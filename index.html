About Us

AVS Development Corporation (AVSDC) is a visionary construction and design company led by UST graduate engineers, architects, and designers. We deliver high-quality, forward-thinking solutions for residential, commercial, and industrial projects across the Philippines.

Mission: To deliver innovative, high-quality, and sustainable construction and design solutions by uniting skilled professionals from all trades—building with integrity, efficiency, and purpose.

Vision: To transform the landscape of construction and design in the Philippines by 2028—leading with excellence, innovating with purpose, and building a more sustainable future.
